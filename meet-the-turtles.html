Meet The Turtles

 

suzzy:

suzzy has been here for 9 months, we found her with a broken leg from it being stuck underneath a rock, we managed to find her before it was too late, she was in terrible condtion but now she is learning to swim again.

moses:

 moses has been here for three months, both of his front legs were broken from him being stuck in a plastic bag, he couldnt move. luckily we managed to find him and was able to save him. he is now resting since he just got out of surgery.

hoops:

hoops has been here for 12 months, he is one of the most hurt turtles, hoops ate 20 packets of plastic, we found him on his back, he was so close to dying but thankfully we managed to do surgery on him, he has overcome it and is now swiming! this was him post surgery.

buddy:

buddy is one of our calmest turtles. we found him when a great white shark attacked him. when we rescued him he stayed calm and he now is better than ever.
